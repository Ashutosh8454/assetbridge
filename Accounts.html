Leverage and Margin Requirements

Leverage gives potential, margin ensures safety. Balance both for smarter trading decisions.

Flexible Leverage Options

Choose leverage ratios from 1:1 to 1:500* based on your risk tolerance and trading strategy. Leverage can be adjusted in your client portal at any time.

Margin Requirements by Asset Class

  • Forex Major Pairs: 0.2% (1:500 leverage)
  • Forex Minor/Exotic Pairs: 0.5% - 2% (varies)

  • Commodities: 1% - 5% (varies)
